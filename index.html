Who I Am

A maker with many hats

I'm Chandan Kumar — an educator, maker, and problem-solver based in Purnea, Bihar. My journey began in cybersecurity & data analysis, evolved through the classroom as a Science & ICT teacher, and has grown into leading technology education at the Head of Department level.

At BOMIS, I don't just teach ICT — I run a Robotics program that puts hardware in students' hands. I believe the best way to learn is to build, break, and rebuild.

When I'm not in the classroom, you'll find me at a workbench, a code editor, or a garden. I weld, woodwork, sketch, edit video, and automate my home with IoT. Curiosity is my operating system.
